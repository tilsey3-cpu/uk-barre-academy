The Difference

The ballet-informed approach.

Most barre training focuses on choreography. This method focuses on technique.

  1. 01
    Alignment first

    Posture and placement come before pace. Every position has a reason.

  2. 02
    Control over repetition

    Quality of movement is the metric. Volume is a by-product, not the goal.

  3. 03
    Precision in every movement

    Cueing, correction, and restraint — taught as a craft, not a script.

  4. 04
    Built on 40+ years of teaching experience

    Distilled from a career in ballet education, applied to the modern barre studio.
